# **App Name**: Grama Prerana

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Impact Narratives: A high-fidelity landing experience highlighting 'hero' stories of local farmers and students using rich media and community-focused storytelling.
- Trust & Transparency Repository: A secure, searchable public folder for 12A, 80G, and annual audit reports stored in the database to establish immediate institutional credibility.
- Bilingual Voice Module: Full-site support for both English and Kannada, allowing for localized cultural nuances including the slogan 'ನಮ್ಮ ಗ್ರಾಮ • ನಮ್ಮ ಅಭಿವೃದ್ಧಿ • ನಮ್ಮ ಭವಿಷ್ಯ'.
- Impact Summary AI Tool: An AI-powered generator that converts complex project field notes and data into heartwarming, concise community impact stories.
- Dynamic Beneficiary CMS: A specialized content management system to update stories, impact counters, and partner logos directly in the cloud database.
- CSR Partnership Portal: A dedicated section for foundations to review governance standards and implementation capacity for specific Sustainable Development Goals.
- Engagement Dashboard: Allows community leaders to track the progress of ongoing programs in education and agriculture through a dashboard visualization.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary: Karnataka Red (#C62828), a saturated, high-contrast red evoking strength and tradition.
- Background: Ivory White (#FFFDF7), providing a warm, organic parchment-like base instead of cold white.
- Accent: Karnataka Yellow (#FBC02D), a vibrant ochre used for high-visibility highlights and calls to action.
- Headlines: 'Literata' (Serif) for a timeless, trustworthy, and literary editorial feel. Body: 'Inter' (Sans-serif) for high readability across demographics.
- Earthy, hand-drawn vector icons and natural textures to emphasize human-centric design over corporate minimalism.
- Generous white space with full-bleed cinematic imagery to ensure people and faces are the central focus of every view.
- Subtle, slow fade-ins for images to evoke a sense of quiet reflection and steady growth.
